Welcome to Pls Give!

Step into a unique multiplayer experience where generosity is exciting, social, and rewarding. Give to others and let them give to you!

  • ⚠️ WARNING: This game uses real Solana transactions. Always review transactions carefully and use a burner wallet for safety. Never approve transactions you don't understand.
  • Controls: Use WASD to move.
  • 1. CLAIM YOUR SPOT Find an empty booth, press "E", then sign in with your wallet to make it yours!
  • 2. GIVE! Visit other players' booths, choose the amount of SOL to give in the donation menu, and send!
  • Get Started: Connect your Phantom Solana wallet to begin.
