Welcome to Pls Give!

⚠️ WARNING: This game uses real Solana transactions. Always review transactions carefully and use a burner wallet for safety. Never approve transactions you don't understand.

Controls: Use WASD to move and right-click to look around.

1. Claim a Spot

Find an empty booth and press "E" to claim it

2. Give to Others!

Visit other players' booths and donate to them

Twitter Integration: Connect your Twitter account to have donations announced on @plsgivealerts!
